About American Renewable Energy

American Renewable Energy is a Houston Texas based, women/family-owned and operated construction and remodeling company. Our number one priority is to make sure that each of our clients receive the highest quality project on time and within budget. We pay close attention to detail and want every project to be perfect right down to the last drop of paint. We oversee every detail of the production of our projects from pre-construction to construction and post-construction services


We have successfully fulfilled contracts with the following Federal Agencies: FBI, DEA, DOS, IRS, CBP, DHS, DLA, EOIR, DOD OIG, TIGTA, USDA, EPA, US Marshall, Federal Courthouses, Diplomatic Security.
